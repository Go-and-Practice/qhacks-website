Co-Chair Application

This year, QHacks was Queen's University's first ever hackathon! QHacks is a 36 hour event where students from all over Canada will be able to turn their ideas into real, tangible projects. In keeping with the quality of the hackathon, we would like to pass on the leadership to the best possible candidates. Some of the QHacks CoChair responsibilities include:

  • Hire and manage a full executive team of 10-14 members.
  • Determine the overall strategy of event execution.
  • Talk to company representatives to acquire partnerships.
