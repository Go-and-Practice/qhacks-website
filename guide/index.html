Joshua Fletcher

Josh has a strong background in delivering ERP solutions focused in the Financial Services Industry. After receiving an undergraduate degree from Queen's University in Biomedical Computing he has had a variety of roles on large scale systems implementations at various global FSI clients, as well as clients in the insurance and retail industries. Through his experience, Josh has built a knowledge base focused on the analysis of impacts systems and technology have on businesses.
Currently, he is working as a Manager at Deloitte Consulting LLP. At Deloitte, his focus has been as a functional consultant working on global financial services ERP projects. His primary roles include a variety of tasks surrounding the Systems Development Life Cycle including project management, operational readiness, cutover planning, business process design, functional & technical workshop facilitation, training & business readiness.

Roger Bowes

Roger graduated from Queen’s University with a B.Sc. Hon. in Electrical Engineering in 1992. He joined Queen’s technology spin-out and start-up ESG Solutions in 1993 managing engineering and product development before taking on the role of CEO from 2006 to 2010. The company was acquired by Spectris plc (LSE:SXS) in 2014 for $64M. In 2012 Roger co-found Laser Depth Dynamics, also a Queen’s technology spin-out and is currently CEO. Other business ventures include BGM Metalworks, Next Urban Group and Annapurna Research. He is also a member of the South-Eastern Ontario Angel Network.
